About This Website

This website serves as a demonstration of various performance optimization techniques, particularly focusing on the impact of lazy loading images. In real-world applications, such optimizations are crucial for improving page load speed and enhancing user experience. Here, you'll find multiple galleries with lazy-loaded images, simulating what might occur on a production website with large media files.
